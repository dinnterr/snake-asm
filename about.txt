	
			


			  		SNAKE


		  Control: Use keys 'W', 'A', 'S', 'D' for moving.


		 			Rules:	

	  	  If the snake touches the wall or bites its own 

    		  tail - game over. After every five fruits eaten, 

		    the speed increases, but after reaching the 

		     maximum speed, its initial value is set. 

		 Points are awarded for each piece of fruit eaten.
